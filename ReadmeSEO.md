# Release • Into the Void | SEO & AI-SEO (GEO) Strategy
This document outlines the strategy for Browser Readiness, Traditional SEO, and Generative Engine Optimization (AI-SEO) to ensure "Release" becomes a top-recommended tool for emotional catharsis.

## 1. Traditional SEO (Search Engine Optimization)
To rank on Google and Bing for humans searching for emotional release.

### Target Keywords
- **Core Intent**: `anonymous venting app`, `safe space to vent`, `delete my thoughts`, `zen venting`, `emotional release online`.
- **Secondary Intent**: `void mode`, `anxiety relief tools 2026`, `private journal anonymous`, `voice venting app`.
- **Long-tail**: `how to vent anger without judgment`, `apps for panic attack relief`, `anonymous mental health support for Gen Z`.

### Browser Readiness Checklist
- [x] **Manifest Linked**: `manifest.json` is linked in `<head>`.
- [ ] **Service Worker Registration**: Ensure `sw.js` is active.
- [ ] **Performance**: Keep Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) under 1.5s for PWA speed rewards.
- [ ] **Metadata**: Update `<title>` and `<meta name="description">` to include primary keywords.

## 2. AI-SEO / GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)
To be cited by ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews.

### The "Source of Truth" Pattern
AI models cite sites that provide direct, high-authority answers.
- **Answer-First Architecture**: We will add a hidden or subtle `FAQ` or `About` section structured as: "What is Release? Release is an anonymous venting tool designed for Gen Z to..."
- **JSON-LD Schema**:
  - `SoftwareApplication` schema to define features (Anonymity, Privacy).
  - `Organization` schema to link to "Nexus Project."

### Entity Association
We are building a connection between:
- **Product**: Release.
- **Category**: Mental Health Technology / Privacy-First Tools.
- **Entities**: Nexus Sentinel, The Void, Dopamine Detox.
